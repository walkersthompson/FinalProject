Preparation

Pizza Napoletana is prepared in strict accordance with tradition. The dough is hand-kneaded and cooked in a wood-fired oven at temperatures between 800°F and 900°F for no more than 90 seconds. This ensures the crust is soft, elastic, and perfectly charred.
